Nooks & Valleys N&V aims to showcase tiny boutique hotels from across the world and feature owner driven hotels that house less than 20 accomodation units only. UX RESEARCH | NOCT - SUMMER INTERNSHIP 2018
Abstract
This project was done under Network Of Creative Thinkers, a Panchgani based Design Agency. Nooks & Valleys aims to showcase hidden gems from different parts of the world along with some interesting nooks/information/insights that might help curious travellers experience truly riveting experiences for their next vacation.
Challenge
Nooks & Valleys focuses on quirky hideouts, targeting the elite and the sumptuous crowd. Thus the client wanted the website to be a blend of a listing portal and a magazine website.
What are Boutique Hotels?
A boutique hotel is a small hotel which typically has between 10 and 100 rooms in unique settings with upscale accommodations and individualized Unique Selling Points (USPs). Many boutique hotels are furnished in a themed, stylish and/or aspirational manner.
